The Modern Museum
Bounty Hunters

Collaboration with Oliver Brooks

This project was a response to a brief set by Bounty Hunters to design a museum exhibiting contemporary and generative art works. The branding begins with the logo, which draws inspiration from the building through its interplay of light and shadow. The letters "M" and "O" cast a shadow that outlines the letters "M" and "U," creating a dynamic mark that highlights the building and the bold creativity represented by the museum.A vibrant colour palette is introduced, using bright RGB selections that emphasize the digital aspect of the brand. Additionally, two shades of each colour are utilized to enhance the theme of light and shadow.The graphic system employs an experimental digital process in its imagery by using code to generate 3D pixelated versions of artworks and the brand itself. This approach underscores MOMU's forward-thinking and experimental stance in the art world.The minimalist typography serves as a counterbalance to the brand's more experiential elements, harmonizing the contemporary and modern styles that MOMU specializes in. Overall, the branding embraces the future of creativity, showcasing a sleek design that invites innovation and exploration.
