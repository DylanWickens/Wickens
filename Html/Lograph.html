Status: Working on it

Tracking workouts should be simple, but existing fitness apps often fall short—they’re either overpriced or poorly designed, leaving many gym-goers to use pen and paper. This is what I've done but it's not as easy as it could be. Navigating notes is clunky, records are often incomplete, and carrying a notebook isn’t always practical. Note apps aren’t much better; they quickly become cluttered and lack the features needed to track workouts efficiently.

To fix my personal issues I’m designing and developing this app solo for my research & development area using React Native, with the goal of creating an intuitive and reliable tool for fitness tracking. The app will be accessible offline, ensuring users can log their workouts seamlessly, even without an internet connection. Its interface will focus on reducing unnecessary steps, allowing users to quickly log sets, reps, and weights. By minimising friction in navigation, the app will encourage consistency and make tracking as effortless as possible
