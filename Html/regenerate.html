Regenerate
Falmouth University

Regenerate is a quarterly magazine celebrating the art of typesetting, published by Falmouth University. For the Spring 2024 issue, I was tasked with designing the entire publication, including its visual identity and typographic layout. The primary goal was to ensure flawless typesetting, showcasing a sophisticated and professional approach to design. I opted for a modernist format, employing a perfect-bound structure to create a polished, timeless aesthetic. Through careful consideration of hierarchy, spacing, and alignment, I made deliberate typesetting decisions that elevated the content while maintaining a cohesive flow throughout the publication.
